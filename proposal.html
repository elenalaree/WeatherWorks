City Weather Comparison App

Overview:

I plan to allow users to search for 2 cities at once so they can compare the weather in each.

Audience:

The audience for this app would be people looking to move to another place or seeing what the weather difference is between two cities.

Major Functions:

  • Pull up 2 cities 5-day forecast side by side for comparison.
  • Ability to save cities as favorites.

Resources:

  • Data Source: OpenWeatherMap
  • Data Storage: Local Storage

Modules:

The app will consist of four main modules:

  • weather.js
  • utils.js
  • favorites.js
  • dayfunction.js
